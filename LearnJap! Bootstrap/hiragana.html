Introduction to Hiragana

1.1 Introduction

Hiragana is the Japanese Alphabets, consisting of 46 basic characters.
Compared to it's counterpart Katakana, which is used for non-japanese sounds or words, Hiragana makes up the foundation of the Japanese Language and is used for writing pure Japanese words
